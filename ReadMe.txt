The CFRP package presents a prototype model for Communicating Functional 
Reactive Processes, or an asynchronizable, effectful FRP model.

The prototype in this package is based on the following work:
* Winograd-Cort, D and Hudak, P. "Communicating Functional Reactive Processes". Unpublished.
* Winograd-Cort, D and Hudak, P. "Wormholes: Introducing Effects to FRP". In Haskell Symposium, pages 91-103. ACM, September 2012.
* Winograd-Cort, D, Liu, H, and Hudak, P. "Virtualizing Real-World Objects in FRP". In Practical Aspects of Declarative Languages, volume 7149 of Lecture Notes in Computer Science, pages 227-241. Springer-Verlag, January 2012.
